# Troubleshooting

Welcome to the troubleshooting page for SimpleCloud v3!
Here, you'll find solutions to common issues and guidance on how to resolve them efficiently.
Whether you're a new user or an experienced developer, this page aims to help you get the most out of SimpleCloud v3.

  Discord](https://discord.simplecloud.app).
